And now, a personal message

In 2017 most of my conversations with Pubby were very one sided. I would talk and he would listen. But I vaguely remember the first time he ended up talking to me. I don’t really remember where we were going, but we were in the train together and he started telling me about his friends and college life and everything. Honestly I felt a sense of achievement that day and pride in the fact that I could connect with Pubby when not many people managed it. Of course over time he’s grown and learnt a lot about himself and made a whole bunch of friends. But back then I was way too excited about the fact that we talked. I actually came back and told my parents and my brother and Jasprit this.

Another significant moment for me with Pubby was when I was in Uttarakhand. I honestly did not consider us keeping in touch. I still considered him to be Jasprit’s brother and not my friend until one day, he called me - just to talk. We talked for a really long time, almost 3 hours. He kept the phone saying call me whenever you need me. Thats when I knew that I was his friend and that was a very important transition for me. I want to thank him for that. Thank him for the fact that even though I’m with his brother who he loves very much, he could connect with me and we could have our own equation. Although we are not the kind who talk to each other everyday, he is one of my closest friends.

There are very few people in my life that I can say will leave everything to come and help out. I know he is one of them. And I know he thinks he wants to “stand alone and carry others”, but if there ever comes a day when standing alone feels a bit dicult, I’ll stand beside him. I know this is a bit cheesy, but that’s okay. I don’t really do this everyday :P.

HAPPY BIRTHDAY PUBBY!!
